Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and correlated issues.

How do I book a consultation session? +

Booking a consultation session is easy! Simply navigate to our "Insurers" section, browse through profiles, and select an agent. From their profile, you can view their availability and book a session directly. You'll receive a confirmation email shortly after.

Are online sessions available? +

Yes, we offer both virtual and in-person consultation sessions to accommodate your preferences and schedule. Our virtual sessions are conducted through a secure, encrypted video platform to ensure your privacy.

How do I make a withdrawal of my pension benefits? +

You can make a withdrawal of your benefits by engaging with your agent or your insurer wherein you will be guided on the withdrawal process.

Do I need a subscription to access services? +

No, a subscription is not required to book individual sessions. However, we do offer various membership plans that provide benefits like discounted rates, access to exclusive resources, and priority booking.

Can I switch agents and insurer if I don't feel the right connection? +

Absolutely. We understand that finding the right intermediary and service provider is crucial for effective service experience. If you feel that your current agent/insurer isn't the best fit, you can easily switch to another professional/provider within our network at any time.

Is my information and session history kept confidential? +

Yes, your data security, privacy and confidentiality are our top priorities. All personal information and session details are kept strictly confidential in accordance with ODPA regulations and other applicable data security compliance and regulation policies.
